Dr. Henry Bright, a retired orthodontist, opened the first O'Henry's in 1993 in Homewood, Alabama, a 1920's suburb of Birmingham. To this day, O'Henry's buys and roasts their own beans at sister company O'Henry's Coffee Roasting Company, also in Homewood. These beans represent the top 1% of the world has to offer.
